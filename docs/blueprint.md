# **App Name**: Homi Care Products

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Display high-quality images and descriptions of Hair Oil, Body Soap, Hair Wash, and Body Care products.
- Detailed Product Pages: Provide in-depth information about each product, including ingredients, benefits, and usage instructions.
- Customer Testimonials: Showcase customer reviews to build trust and credibility.
- Contact Form: Allow users to send questions and messages directly through the site.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Pure white (#FFFFFF) to create a clean and calm feel.
- Primary accent color: Olive green (#566336) for a natural and premium look.
- Secondary accent colors: Light olive shades, soft beige, and muted greens to complement the primary color.
- Headings font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with an elegant and high-end feel; use 'PT Sans' for longer text.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans', a clean, readable sans-serif.
- Use leaf, oil drop, and herbal icons with soft olive highlights to represent natural ingredients and benefits.
- Incorporate plenty of white space to create a calm and trustworthy feel.
- Use a hero section with a bold headline, short tagline, CTA buttons ('Shop Now' / 'Explore Products'), and minimal product mockups with soft shadows.
- Organize product cards in a clean grid with product images, names, benefit lines, and 'View Details' buttons.
- Implement smooth hover effects on buttons and subtle animations for a premium feel.
